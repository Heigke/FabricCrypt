# H4 Pre-registration — Phase-2 equivalence (TOST formal null re-analysis)

Date committed: 2026-06-09. Author: research agent (Claude), OK'd by Eric.
Hash-stamp: SHA-256 at commit is the pre-registration anchor.

## Question
On commodity x86 APU user-space (Strix Halo + ROCm + Linux), is the HW-conditioned Δ formally equivalent to the SHUFFLE control Δ within ±0.5% under TOST at α=0.05 and 1−β=0.9 on the existing Phase-2 dataset?

## Hypothesis (H4)
H4_null: |Δ_HW − Δ_SHUFFLE| < 0.5% (i.e., HW channel is equivalent to a random permutation w.r.t. identity signal).
H4_alt:  |Δ_HW − Δ_SHUFFLE| ≥ 0.5% (HW channel carries real identity signal).

## Data source (frozen)
- `results/IDENTITY_BENCHMARK_2026-05-30/phase2/matrix_results.json`
- 60 rows: 2 devices (ikaros, daedalus) × 3 controls (HW, SW_iid, SHUFFLE) × 10 seeds.
- nrmse is the response variable.

## Bounds and decisions (pre-registered)
- Equivalence bound Δ_low = −0.005, Δ_high = +0.005 (±0.5% nrmse).
- α = 0.05 (one-sided TOST p-values; reject H4_alt if BOTH p_lower < 0.05 AND p_upper < 0.05).
- Bayes factor: report BF_10 comparing chassis-only model (effect ~ device) vs full model (effect ~ device + control). Decision-threshold BF_10 < 1/3 = evidence for null.
- Multiple comparisons: BH q=0.05 across (a) the TOST, (b) per-device delta tests, (c) per-control variance tests.

## Power analysis (pre-registered)
- Stated MDE at n=10 seeds/cell, σ from observed data: compute and report.
- If MDE > 0.005 at α=0.05, 1−β=0.9, report "underpowered — re-collect" rather than claim equivalence.

## Acceptance gates
- PASS_NULL: TOST rejects H4_alt (both p_lower<0.05 AND p_upper<0.05) AND BF_10<1/3 vs chassis-confound model AND MDE ≤ 0.005.
- PASS_ALT: TOST fails to reject AND |observed Δ| > 0.005 AND BF_10>3.
- UNDETERMINED: MDE > 0.005 — recollect with required n.

## What we will NOT do
- No re-selection of seeds.
- No equivalence bound widening after seeing point estimate.
- No claim of "no signal" if MDE > 0.005 — that's underpowered, not null.
- No swap of nrmse for a different DV.
